<p> AL-FAHAM CHICKEN </p>

ABOUT

"Faham is an Arabic word for coal and the name comes from this word. Al-Faham is basically the Arabian barbecued chicken usually grilled over coal or in oven. Although this dish originated in the Middle East, it is now more popular in India, especially Kerala."

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken - 1 kg
  • Curd - 1/4 cup
  • Salt - 2 tsp
  • Black pepper powder - 2tsp
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Lemon juice - 2 nos
  • Cardamon - 5 nos
  • Cloves - 6 nos
  • Corriander seeds - tbsp
  • Cumin - 1/2 tsp
  • Fennel seeeds - 1 tsp
  • Dry kasmiri chilly - 6 to 8 nos
  • Tomato - 1 no
  • Onion (small sized) - 1 no
  • Green chilly - 1
  • Corriander leaves - 1/4 cup
  • Oil - 3 tbsp

STEPS TO PREPARE

  1. Take 1 kg of whole chicken, slits on the chicken breast to flatten it and small cuts all around.
  2. For marination : add curd,salt,black pepper powder,ginger garlic paste and lemon in t the bowl and mix it well.Apply the mixture to the chicken and rest it for 30 min - 2 hr.
  3. Take the Pan add cardamon,clove,coriander seeds,cumin,fennel seeds and kasmiri chilly then roast these spices on low heat.
  4. Add the roasted items in to the grinder and grind it to he fine powder.
  5. Add tomato,onion,green chilly,corriander leaves and 1 tbsp oil in to the grinder along with the pre-grinder powder and grind all together.
  6. Add these grinded marination all over the chicken and keep it in the refrigerator for 2 hr - Overnight.
  7. After resting time of chicken, Take a pan and add 2 tbsp oil and place a chicken on it. Cook it for 10 min on both sides then cover the chicken with the lid and cook it for 15 - 20 min on medium-low heat. Once the chicken is dry remove it from the pan.
  8. Place the chicken on the grill or Pulka maker directly on the flame and smoke it on both sides
